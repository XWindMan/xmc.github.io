### 图片不同区域点击跳转不同连接
+ 连接map	
+ 设置map	
+ 设置不同的area	
+ 设置坐标 coords

```
<img src="./img/bg-home.jpg" alt="" usemap="#map">
<map name="map" id="map">
     <area shape="poly" coords="583,382,587,357,609,359,611,283,1014,247,1392,352,1362,410,1218,445,583,382" href="https://www.baidu.com/" alt="">
```
